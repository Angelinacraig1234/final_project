Broccoli Noodle Salad with Asian Peanut Citrus Sauce
20 minutes| 820 cal

This recipe is a different and interesting style for salads. It can be eaten for both lunch and dinner. It is not a heavy dish, which means it can be eaten with sides of your choice. So enjoy this Asian turn on salad.

16 oz whole grain spaghetti

1/4 cup Pure Canola Oil, 1 tablespoon

1/2 cup Creamy Peanut Butter

1/3 cup orange juice

3 tablespoons lemon juice

1/2 cup soy sauce

2 tablespoons sugar

1 cup sliced green onions

1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper

3 tablespoons minced garlic

1 small jalapeno pepper, seeds and ribs removed, minced

2 cups broccoli florets, split if large

bonus you can add chopped onion, mushroom and red pepper for an extra kick.

Instructions:

Cook pasta in salted water, according to package directions; drain well. Place the spaghetti in large bowl and toss with 1 tablespoon canola oil. Set aside. Whisk peanut butter, orange juice, lemon juice, soy sauce and sugar in medium bowl until blended well. Heat 1/4 cup oil in a large, deep skillet over medium high heat.
