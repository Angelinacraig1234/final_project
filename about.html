Easy Eatz! Health Tips

Let's Get That Health On!

This section of our website is to provide health tips.

Adolescence is a time of growth for children, for both physical and mental development. A healthy diet is essential for teenagers because of their heightened nutritional needs, but also because the foods they eat as teenagers can impact their health when they reach adulthood. Healthy eating also promotes good food habits to last a lifetime.

Here are some Tips to Stay Healthy!!



Try to drink 8 cups of water everyday. Even though we may not need 8 cups of water it definetly helps

  • balance maintain
  • balance of body fluid
  • clearer skin
  • things flow easier
  • calorie control

If you are not too fond of drinking water you can add fruit and mint to it, it definetly enhances the water. There is a recipe for this in our recipe section.


Make sure your body gets the vitamins it needs. If you have any dietary restrictions or health problems take the supplements required. Taking vitamins help:

  • support healthy aging
  • boost energy levels
  • detox your body
  • improve your skin
  • boost mood
  • manage stress

When taking vitamins, make sure to add fish oil. Fish oil has been proven to

  • improve skin
  • increase energy
  • lifts depression
  • thickens hair

When talking about the amount of calories you need to take everyday you have to keep in mind everyone is different. The amount of calories needed depends on your

  • age
  • height
  • gender
  • daily activity level
  • depending on if you want to maintain, gain or lose weight

When it comes to snacks you want to make sure you stay away from junk food. So put down those chips and swap them for a healthy alternative. If you're craving something sweet go for fruits, fruits give you many health benefits. You can also have a salad if craving something semi salty. If you don't have any of those things available you can eat granola or granola bars. Make sure that these granola bars are low in calories.


Exercise, that dreaded word many of us don't like. The thing is exercise depends your age and weight. You don't necessarily have to do intense work outs. You can go for a walk for a certain amount of time. You can do so many things for example instead of taking the bus or a taxi to a short destination you can walk to the area.


When eating you should'nt eat past 9 because that can cause over eating and it wouldnt give your body enough time to digest.Sleep is very important. The amount of sleep would show throughout the day and make sure to get as much sleep as possible. It is good to get at least 8 hours of sleep. Sleep is very important for growth in adolescence
