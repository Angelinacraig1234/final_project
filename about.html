Let's Get That Health On!

This section of our website is to provide health tips.

Adolescence is a time of growth for children, for both physical and mental development. A healthy diet is essential for teenagers because of their heightened nutritional needs, but also because the foods they eat as teenagers can impact their health when they reach adulthood. Healthy eating also promotes good food habits to last a lifetime.

Here are some Tips to Stay Healthy!!

Try to drink ten cups of water a day. Make sure your body gets the vitamins it needs. If you have any dietary restrictions, take the supplements required. When taking vitamins, make sure to have fish oil. You should have 150 grams of protein three times a day. For a snack, eat a veggie salad or fruit salad. Get exercise daily. Walk to places instead of driving. Here are examples of healthy food: cottage cheese, chicken, oatmeal, honey, vegetables, fruit, soup. Make sure not to eat anything past 7 pm. Sleep is very important and make sure to get as much of it as possible. It is also important to rest during the night for example 9-7am.
