Avocado Toast with Fried Egg
|16 minutes|

Avocado toast with fried egg! Breakfast is served. Yummy and totally satisfying.

Ingredients:

1 avocado

2 slices toasted bread

1/2 tsp olive oil

2 large eggs

1 small tomato

1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper

salt and pepper

Instructions:

Dice, then scoop avocado into a small bowl. Add Â½ tsp olive oil, pinch of salt and cayenne pepper. Mash with a fork. Meanwhile, toast bread slices. Fry eggs sunny side up (or as you like). Spread avocado on toast. Add alfalfa sprouts and sliced tomato. Cover with fried egg. Add additional spices as desired.
