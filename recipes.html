Hummus and Grilled Veggie Wrap
23 minutes| 320 cal|

This is a fast and easy wrap, enjoy the wonderful combination of hummus and veggies on a whole wheat wrap. This is a dish you can accommodate to your taste. If you prefer to add chicken then add chicken.

Whole wheat tortilla

Grilled zucchini, cut lengthwise(grill time 4minutes)

Grilled peppers, thinly sliced

washed spinach

2 teaspoon of olive oil

1/8 teaspoon of salt

Hummus of your choice

red onion thinly sliced

Quesadilla
15 minutes| 224 cal|

Quesadilla are a very fast and yummy dish that you can change up to your own taste. You can play around with different stuffings. For example different cheeses, meats, veggies and spices, as long as they are all the right portions. This recipe right here is a simple quesadilla recipe.

1 Tbsp coconut oil, divided

1 cup chopped onion

oregano and cilantro

1 clove garlic, chopped

1 tsp chili powder

shredded cheddar cheese (amount is up to you)

Basil, Mozzarella and Tomato Sandwich
15 minutes| 365 cal|

This delicious sandwich is flavorfull and easy to make. Definitely for those who love mozzarella.

Ciabatta bread

1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil

4 ounces sliced fresh mozzarella

thin tomato slices (enough for your bread and depeding on your taste)

salt and freshly ground pepper

8 large basil leaves
