Baby Lettuce with Strawberries and Almonds
15 minutes| 100 cal|

If you love salad and strawberries why not combine the two. Adding strawberries gives the salad a kick that you would love. The almonds in this dish give a perfect crunch to the salad.

2 cupsbaby baby lettuce

strawberries cut in half(desired amount)

grapes(desired amount)

extra virgin olive oil

smoked or roasted almonds(desired amount)

honey

apple cider vinegar or balsomic vinegar

sliced white onion

feta

Instructions:

In a small bowl, stir together honey, vinegar and the sliced onion together. After that stir in the olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Put the lettuces in a large bowl. Add the strawberries, feta, almonds and grapes. Drizzle the dressing over the salad, toss well and serve.
