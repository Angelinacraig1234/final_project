Quesadilla
15 minutes| 224 cal|

Quesadilla are a very fast and yummy dish that you can change up to your own taste. You can play around with different stuffings. For example different cheeses, meats, veggies and spices, as long as they are all the right portions. This recipe right here is a simple quesadilla recipe.

1 Tbsp coconut oil, divided

oregano and cilantro

1 clove garlic, chopped

1 tsp chili powder

1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

shredded cheddar cheese (amount is up to you)

4 whole-wheat tortillas

Instructions:

Warm a non stick pan over low heat. Take one tortilla and sprinkle some of the cheddar cheese on the tortilla. Try not to get the cheese too close to the edge. Then add the chopped garlic, chili powder and black pepper. Work quickly and after you're done add the other tortilla on top and press lightly. After that using a spatula, gently lift to check, what you should see is a slight brown on the bottom of the tortilla. Turn the tortilla over and let that side cook until it also turns brown and the cheese is all goey. Use the spatula to transfer the quesadilla to a plate. A side dish that you can have with this quesadilla are cucumber salad, guacamole or salsa.
